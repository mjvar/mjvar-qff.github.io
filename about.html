About Quarantine Film Festival

Our Mission

Film festivals have historically served as cultural hubs, avenues for filmmakers to showcase the fruits of their labor. With the onset of remote life, we want to provide an even greater platform for filmmakers to bring quality cinema to people around the world. Quarantine Film Festival celebrates films characteristic of our day and age, films that embrace our "new normal" and challenge us to imagine new ways of adapting.

Our Vision

We want to redefine the norms surrounding film festivals. We hold a firm belief in the accessibility and openness of cinema, and an optimism that our commitment to these values will set positive precedents for other festivals moving forward. We envision a world where filmmakers are empowered to share their work with people around the world, where conversations surrounding cinema are global and timely, and where quality cinema is accessible to all.
