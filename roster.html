Film Roster

New Friends

Safeya, Aiša, Hasin, Matthew

New Friends follows the story of 4 individuals in quarantine around the world. Kept at home with nothing to do, the protagonists find unexpected ways to keep themselves company during quarantine. A fascinating exploration of the isolated soul, New Friends begs important questions about our collective quarantine psyche.

Other Film

Ishmal, Rashid, Hessa, Amina

Other Film is an adrenaline-filled action-horror masterpiece that challenges the norms of the horror genre. Set in sunny NYU Abu Dhabi, the film's bright cinematography follows in the footsteps of horror film greats such as Midsommar by Ari Aster. Other Film is the jarring and idiosyncratic period piece that defines our generation.

Yet Another Film

Loan, Fatima, Alpha, Ayesha

Yet Another Film is a riveting documentary that sheds light on the struggle of inanimate objects living in college dormitories. A sharply realistic exploration of object exploitation at the hands of international university students, Yet Another Film is a necessary and painful wake-up call to society.

And Lastly, One More Film

Mike, Shamma, Shreya, Soojin

And Lastly, One More Film deftly mixes elements of punchy bildungsroman and romantic comedy. The film expertly narrates the metamorphosis of its protagonist, Black Jacket, in a heart-fluttering blockbuster certain to captivate fans of the romantic genre. And Lastly, One More Film is a masterfully written love letter to classic films like Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind and Cars 2.
